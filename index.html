Specifications

  • 01. STRUCTURE
    • Conventional Column beam structure with masonry walls - Seismic Zone compliant structural design.
  • 02. DOORS
    • MAIN DOOR: Teak wood frame and engineered shutter with veneer - PU polished on both sides.
    • OTHER DOORS: Hardwood frame and engineered shutter as per factory finish.
    • TOILET DOORS: Hardwood frame and engineered shutter with laminate finish on both sides.
    • BALCONY DOORS: Glazed 2.5 track UPVC frames & sliding shutters with provision for insect mesh.
    • UTILITY DOORS: UPVC partially glazed sliding/hinged shutter.
  • 03. WINDOWS
    • Glazed 2.5 track UPVC frames &sliding shutters with provision for insect mesh.
  • 04. VENTILATORS
    • Glazed UPVC frames with louvers and provision for exhaust fans.
  • 05. FLOORING
    • LIVING & DINING ROOM: Vitrified tiles
    • MASTER BEDROOM: 8mm thick Laminated wooden flooring
    • OTHER BEDROOMS: Vitrified tiles
    • KITCHEN: Vitrified tiles
    • BALCONY/UTILITY: Antiskid ceramic tiles
    • TOILETS: Antiskid ceramic tiles
  • 06. WALLS
    • INTERNAL WALLS: Emulsion Paint finish.
    • KITCHEN: Supply of granite counter and above counter dado tiles
    • TOILETS: Ceramic tiles upto false ceiling
    • BALCONY: External grade weather proof paint.
    • UTILITY: Emulsion paint finish.
  • 07. STAIRCASE
    • Granite Flooring (Basement to Ground Levels) Kota/Tandoor stone (for upper Levels)
  • 08. CEILINGS
    • ALL INTERNAL CEILINGS: Emulsion Paint finish.
    • TOILETS: PVC gypsum laminated modular grid false ceiling
    • ENTRANCE LOBBY: Gypsum board false ceiling as per Architect's design
  • 09. SANITARY FITTINGS
    • RAK, Parryware or equivalent.
  • 10. CP FITTINGS
    • Jaguar or equivalent.
  • 11. ELECTRICAL
    • Anchor or Equivalent.
  • 12. OTHER SERVICES
    • ELEVATORS: Reputed Brand Automatic elevators with emergency call facility.
    • INTERCOM FACILITY: Living/Dining of each apartment connected to security
    • WIFI FACILITY: CAT 5 cable provision for point at Living/Dining
    • CCTV: at designated locations as per design
